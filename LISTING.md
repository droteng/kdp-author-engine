### Body

**Stop doing everything yourself.**

Writing a book is one thing. Researching the market, outlining, drafting, editing, formatting for KDP, writing the book description, researching keywords, picking categories, setting up ads, planning the launch, managing ARC readers, tracking sales — that's a full-time publishing operation.

KDP Author Engine gives you a team of 6 AI agents that handle the entire pipeline:

**Bookfinder** 🔎 scans Amazon every week and delivers 3 validated title recommendations with full keyword intelligence — backend keywords, BISAC categories, competitive analysis, and revenue estimates. You pick the winner.

**Author** 📖 turns your approved title into a detailed blueprint with chapter outlines, audience targeting, and tone guides. Then manages the entire writing process chapter by chapter.

**Bookwriter** ✍️ drafts each chapter following strict humanization rules. No AI-sounding prose. No "delve" or "tapestry." Every chapter reads like a skilled human author wrote it.

**Editor** ✏️ scores every draft on 6 dimensions — Language Quality, Humanization, Genre Compliance, Engagement, Continuity, and Structure. Nothing passes below 7.5/10. Failed chapters go back with specific revision instructions.

**Publisher** 🚀 formats your manuscript for KDP and IngramSpark. Builds the complete metadata package — description, keywords, categories, pricing. Produces a cover brief. Runs the pre-upload checklist.

**Marketer** 📣 executes your launch — ARC reader recruitment, Amazon Ads campaigns, social media content, email sequences. Then tracks performance at 30, 60, and 90 days with full reports.

### Key Differentiators

**Not just a writing tool.** Most AI book tools stop at "generate text." KDP Author Engine covers the full pipeline — market validation, writing, editing, publishing, and marketing. Your book is market-validated before a single word is written.

**Anti-AI-detection built in.** The humanization engine isn't a final-pass filter. It's embedded in every agent's instructions. Bookwriter self-reviews. Editor scores. Author approves. Three layers of human-quality assurance.

**Keyword intelligence flows downstream.** Bookfinder researches keywords once. Author uses them in the blueprint. Publisher uses them in KDP metadata. Marketer uses them in ad campaigns. No one starts from scratch.

**You stay in control.** Every chapter requires your approval. Every title requires your sign-off. The agents do the work. You make the decisions.
